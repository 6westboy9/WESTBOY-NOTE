
可大体分为两种：

* JVM<font color="#e36c09">启动时</font>静态加载
* JVM<font color="#e36c09">启动后</font>动态加载

# 启动时静态加载

## 参数设置

<font color="#f79646">写法一</font>

```
-javaagent:xxx.jar={k1=v1},{k2=v2}
-javaagent:xxx.jar=service.include=xxx,service.exclude=xxx
```

<font color="#f79646">写法二</font>

```
-javaagent:xxx.jar -D{k1=v1} -D{k1=v2}
-javaagent:xxx.jar -Dservice.include=xxx -Dservice.exclude=xxx
```
